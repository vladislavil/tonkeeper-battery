Tonkeeper Battery

Swap via Tonkeeper, send tokens and NFTs

What is Tonkeeper Battary?

The battery of the Tonkipper makes life much easier for new users: you can put a wallet, get a stablecoin or NFT, and immediately start using them, without using tones to pay for gas. You do not need to register in exchangers and undergo document checks only to buy an incomprehensible amount of coins to pay for an incomprehensible gas.

  • Gas-free

    Gas-free transactions in USDT, Notcoin and other tokens

  • NFT

    NFT transfers

  • Replenishment

    Replenishment of the balance through in-app purchases or cryptocurrency

How it work?

With the help of a battery, you can pay with tokens (stablecoins, NFT, etc.) without having to buy tones in exchangers to pay for blockchain commissions. The battery pays the commission for you automatically.

You can charge the battery by card using the built-in payments on iOS or using tones or stablecoins. Send a few dollars in stablecoins or tones to a special battery.ton address (https://tonviewer.com/battery.ton ) — The battery will be charged for your wallet.

The battery of the Tonkeeper is measured in charges, each charge covers the commission for a simple TON transfer transaction. More complex transactions may require 5, 10 or more charges.

How to use it?

  • Step 1

    To use the battery, you must log in to Tonkeeper → Settings → Battery

  • Step 2

    Choose a payment method

  • Step 3 - Payment in TON

    Select the payment amount in TON. The possible options are presented below

  • Step 3 - Payment in USDT

    Select the payment amount in USDT. The possible options are presented below

  • Done!

    After a hasty paymet, an icon will appear on the main page of the Tonkeeper application batteries. This means that the battery is charged.

  • Number of charges

    To view the remaining charges, you can use the steps from Step 1 or click on the battery next to the amount on the wallet balance on the main page.
